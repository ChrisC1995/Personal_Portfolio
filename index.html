Restuarant Roulette

Restaurant Roulette was a project that was made to assist local businesses in Eastern Kentucky. A user picks their craving and the system randomly selects a restaurant akin to their tastes. Challenges in this app included learning Firebase and implementing the variety of random methods based on cravings. It was overall a great experience. I learned a variety of contents about Firebase, randomization, and mapping skills.

Gift.It

Gift.It is an app that was created to help individuals during struggles financially. A user can view other individuals needs and commit to providing them. The app is completely anonymous. Challenges include working with parse and including the anonymity factor. I learned a great deal about parse, asynchronous calls, and using application classes to initialize processes.

Togglit

Togglit is an app that I contributed to. I actively refactored various parts of it and adapted it to current specifications. This app was used by Interapt as an app customizer and creator for companies.

Scribblit

Scribblit is an app that I contributed to. It is a HIPPA compliant messaging app that allows users to message, announce, or converse with teams. This app presented me with great learning oppertunities that include: Model View Presenter Architechture, RxJava, and Dagger2.

Kunc (Colorado Sound)

Kunc (Colorado Sound) is an app that I contributed to. I refactored certain portions of the app to fit current standards in android development. I also integrated a push notification feature onto the app.
